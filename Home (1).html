Welcome

Kyankima Women's Cooperative is a community-based organization founded by 47 women in Mukono District, Uganda. We focus on small-scale agriculture, handcraft production, and community health awareness.

About Us

Founded in 2019, our cooperative supports women through product development, health tips, and community outreach. We pride ourselves on empowering women and promoting sustainable development.
